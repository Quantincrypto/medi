## KEY FINDINGS — What the Data Revealed

### 1. Equipment Pricing Pages Are a Major Opportunity (Not in Original 22)
Kenya searchers want **prices**, not just "suppliers". These are high-volume, transactional:
- "ultrasound machine price in kenya" — **260/mo**
- "x ray machine price in kenya" — **210/mo**
- "mri machine price in kenya" — **170/mo**
- "portable ultrasound machine price in kenya" — **70/mo**
- "cost of ultrasound machine in kenya" — **20/mo**

**Action:** Create individual equipment pricing pages. These keywords have no equivalent competitor page and attract buyers at the point of purchase decision.

### 2. City-Level Pages Are Essential
"Medical equipment suppliers in [city]" terms all have real volume:
- "medical equipment suppliers in nairobi" — 140/mo
- "medical equipment suppliers in lagos nigeria" — 140/mo
- "medical equipment suppliers in johannesburg" — 170/mo
- "medical equipment suppliers pretoria" — 170/mo
- "medical equipment suppliers in abuja" — 70/mo
- "medical equipment suppliers in gauteng" — 110/mo (KD: 51 — harder)

**Action:** Dedicated city-level landing pages for Nairobi, Lagos, Abuja, Johannesburg, Pretoria.

### 3. "Near Me" Signal = Google Business Profile Priority
- "medical equipment suppliers near me" — **210/mo** (KD: 29)

This is a local pack trigger. Winning this keyword requires GBP optimisation, not just a web page.

### 4. South Africa = Highest Value Market by CPC
All SA supplier terms cluster at **$1.10 CPC** — more than double Kenya ($0.53) and Nigeria ($1.04). This signals commercial depth. SA should be treated as a premium traffic market.

### 5. Lab Equipment = Separate Cluster
- "laboratory equipment suppliers kenya" — 170/mo
- "laboratory equipment suppliers in kenya" — 170/mo
- "medical laboratory equipment suppliers in nigeria" — 10/mo ($2.69 CPC — highest in Nigeria)
- "medical laboratory equipment suppliers in south africa" — 10/mo ($2.31 CPC)

**Action:** Dedicated lab equipment page. High CPC = high commercial intent even at 10/mo.

### 6. Wholesale/Bulk Buyers Exist
- "wholesale medical supplies kenya" — **110/mo**
- "medical supplies price list" — **210/mo**

These are procurement-level buyers. Create a catalogue/price-list page targeting these.

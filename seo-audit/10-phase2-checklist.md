## Step 2 — Metrics to Capture for Each Keyword

For each keyword in the list above, record:

| Metric | What to look for |
|---|---|
| **Monthly search volume** | Target: 100+ globally, even 10–50 is worth targeting in niche B2B |
| **Keyword difficulty (KD)** | Under 30 = easy win; 30–50 = achievable; 50+ = long game |
| **CPC (cost per click)** | High CPC = high commercial intent. $1–5+ for B2B medical = very valuable |
| **SERP features present** | Featured snippet? People Also Ask? Local pack? Images? Each is an opportunity. |
| **Trend direction** | Is volume growing or declining over 12 months? |
| **Current ranking** | Where does medicalequipment.africa currently appear (if at all)? |
| **Top 3 competitors ranking** | Which domains? What DA/DR do they have? Are they beatable? |

## Step 3 — Finding New Keywords from Semrush

### Method A: Keyword Magic Tool
1. Enter root keyword: "medical equipment Africa"
2. Filter by: Questions, Broad Match
3. Sort by: Volume descending
4. Look for long-tail variations not in the current list (especially question-format queries)

### Method B: Competitor Gap
1. Enter competitor domain: medhospafrica.com
2. Run "Keyword Gap" against medicalequipment.africa
3. Filter: Keywords competitor ranks for that the site does NOT
4. These are the fastest-win opportunities — steal their rankings

### Method C: Topic Clusters
1. Enter each root keyword (ultrasound, CT scanner, MRI) into Semrush Keyword Magic
2. Export all related keywords
3. Group by page intent: informational (buying guide) vs. transactional (category page)
4. Identify cluster topics that can become individual pages or sections

## Step 6 — What to Do If a Keyword Has Zero Volume

**Zero volume ≠ worthless.** Consider these scenarios:

| Scenario | What to do |
|---|---|
| Keyword is too specific but commercially relevant | Create the page anyway. Verified buyers search these exact phrases. One $50k conversion justifies 50 zero-volume keyword pages. |
| Keyword is a question format with zero volume | Use it as an H2 heading or FAQ question within a higher-volume page instead of a standalone page |
| Keyword has zero volume in Semrush but high in-country volume | Check Keywords Everywhere with geo-filter set to Kenya/Nigeria specifically. African markets are under-indexed in Western SEO tools. |
| Keyword has zero volume but high CPC | CPC > $2 with zero recorded volume = Google Ads data gap. Create the page — paid advertisers are targeting it. |
| Keyword is a regulatory term (PPB, NAFDAC) | Always create this content. Regulatory content attracts professional buyers with very high intent, regardless of recorded volume. |

**General rule:** In African B2B markets, Semrush frequently under-reports search volumes because its data is weighted toward US/UK/European queries. Trust your buyer knowledge over the tool.
